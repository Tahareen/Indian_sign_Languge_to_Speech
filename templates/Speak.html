How to Use:

  • Show sign language gestures: The system will detect and recognize Indian Sign Language.
  • Blink to capture: When you blink, the current detected gesture will be added to the text buffer.
  • Add Space: Click the button to add a space between words.
  • Backspace: Remove the last character from the buffer.
  • Speak Text: Convert the buffer text to speech.
  • Clear All: Empty the text buffer completely.
